Here are the Bristol Balloon Fiesta posters I designed.

This was my first design for the Bristol balloon fiesta. Itâ€™s the task I spent the most time on it because I had no idea how and what to design a poster at the beginning. I did lots of research on designing a poster. I used google to find different kinds of posters to draw some inspiration. Besides, I have learned the basics of using Adobe InDesign while designing the poster as It is my first time using Adobe InDesign. I learned it from google and YouTube too.

In my first design, you can see a painting of the sky in the background and I put a hot air balloon in the centre of the whole page because it is the main object that I want people to notice when they see this poster. The name of the festival is on top of the balloon. The information and the sponsor are at the bottom.

In my second design, you can see the style of it is totally different from the first one. I used two triangles to make some layers on the bottom and add a rectangular strip that paralleled the triangle on the bottom right. On the top, I used one of the images that I took in Clifton bridge. I think it is beautiful and suitable for my poster, especially the sunset. Then, I put the name of the festival on the top left. Sponsors are inside the triangle on the bottom left and information is on the bottom right.

In my third design, I also put the balloon in the centre of the poster, but the balloon is way bigger than the first one. I put most of the things inside the balloon. The name of the festival, information and sponsors. I only put the date and the cost on the bottom left and make it black because it is a piece of important information.
